Publications

Registration → Publications

The Technical Program Committee shall comprise of experts drawn from different areas of Power, Energy and Energy Management, Dielectrics, Electrical Drives and Industrial Applications area.


· Complete manuscripts shall be reviewed.


· The Conference shall be using blind review system.


· The decision on final acceptance of a paper will lie with the Technical Program Committee.


· The Conference Publication Committee shall communicate with the authors of accepted papers to ensure camera-ready presentation materials.


· Accepted paper shall be considered for inclusion in the Conference Publication only after it is presented in the Conference. At least one author of
  the accepted paper shall have to present the paper.


· An author shall be allowed to present a maximum of two accepted papers.


· The Conference Publication shall be included in the IEEE Conference Publication Program (CPP) which includes publication in IEEE Xplore.
